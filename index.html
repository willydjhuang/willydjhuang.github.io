Ding-Jiun Huang

I'm Ding-Jiun Huang, a Master's student in Computer Vision (MSCV) at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Prof. Fernando de la Torre. My current research focuses on enhancing 3D dynamic humans, such as super-resolving a 3D head avatar trained from low-quality capturings. Prior to this, I had the previlege to be advised by Prof. Yu-Chiang Frank Wang and Dr. Cheng Sun from NVIDIA Research.

I completed my undergraduate studies at National Taiwan University, and had the pleasure to work with Prof. Chung-Wei Lin in my undergraduate research for autonomous vehicles motion planning.
