I'm Ding-Jiun Huang (黃定鈞), a student researcher working in [Vision and Learning Lab](http://vllab.ee.ntu.edu.tw/), advised by ​​Prof. [Yu-Chiang Frank Wang](http://vllab.ee.ntu.edu.tw/ycwang.html), and I am fortunate to have worked with Dr. [Cheng Sun](https://sunset1995.github.io/) from NVIDIA Research. My research interests include 3D computer vision, image/video processing and autonomous vehicles.

I received my B.S. from Department of [Computer Science and Information Engineering](https://www.csie.ntu.edu.tw//?locale=en), [National Taiwan University](https://www.ntu.edu.tw/english/) in 2023, and I had the pleasure to conduct research under the supervision of Prof. [Chung-Wei Lin](https://www.csie.ntu.edu.tw/~cwlin/) in my undergraduate researches.
